# Alchemy: The Last Hope

An evil warlord has seized the kingdom and imprisoned the Queen deep within the dungeons below.  
As the last living alchemist, you alone possess the power to turn fallen monsters into potions of might and magic.  
Brew your way through the darkness and save the Queen before it’s too late.

## Survival Guide for Alchemists

- Move using **WASD** or the **arrow keys**. Stay mobile.
- Smash that **Space** or **Enter** key to attack.
- Drag the view to spot hazards before they kill you.
- Build energy as you fight, then hit **S** to go She-Hulk and smash hard.
